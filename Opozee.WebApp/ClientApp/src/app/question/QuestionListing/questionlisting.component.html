Welcome to Opozee!

Our mission is to build a decentralized open-source social network that helps users converge to shared beliefs and promotes conversation health.

Think of us as Instagram for sharing and discussing beliefs.

How Opozee works:
Step 1: Sign up and join the community!
Step 2: Post a “yes/no” question or your beliefs in response to existing questions.
Step 3: Earn Opozee Coins (OPZ) when people up-vote your beliefs.
What’s in it for me:

500 Opozee Coins (OPZ) for signing up and earn more as you participate in the network.

Every OPZ coin holder is a shareholder in the network with a claim on the network's growth and profitability. As the value of the network increases, so will the value of OPZ coins.
