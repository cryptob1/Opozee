Welcome to Opozee!

Our mission is to build a decentralized open-source social network that offers a platform for free speech and encourages users to converge to shared beliefs. Think of us as Instagram for sharing and discussing beliefs.

How Opozee works:
Step 1: Sign up and join the community!
Step 2: Post a “yes/no” question or your belief in response to existing questions.
Step 3: Earn Opozee Coins (OPZ) when people like your beliefs.
What’s in it for me?

You get 500 Opozee Coins (OPZ) just for signing up. You can earn additional OPZ coins by actively participating in the network by posting beliefs. Every OPZ coin holder is a shareholder in the network with a claim on the network's growth and profitability. As the value of the networks increases, so will the value of its tokens.
