Welcome to Opozee!

Our mission is to be the worlds first decentralized social network that offers a platform for free speech and ecourages users to converge to shared beliefs.

How Opozee works:
Step 1: Start by posting a “yes or no” question.
Step 2: Or simply post your beliefs in response to existing questions.
Step 3: Earn crypto-tokens when people like your beliefs.
What’s in there for me?

You get 100 tokens just for signing up. You can earn additional tokens by actively participating in the network by posting beliefs. Opozee plans to operate as a decentralized autonomous organization (DOA). Every Opozee tokenholder is shareholder in the network with a claim on the networks growth and profitability. As the value of the networks increases, so will the value of its tokens.
