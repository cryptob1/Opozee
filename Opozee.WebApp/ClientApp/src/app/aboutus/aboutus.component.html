About Us

Opposing Viewpoints · 400 characters · Outrage Free
Decentralized · User Owned


Opozee is a decentralized and troll-free social platform where users can post opposing viewpoints in 400 characters or less.

Opozee provides a safe space where users can share and vote on viewpoints without inflammatory and digressive attacks (no trolls!). Opozee is decentralized and user-owned. Users earn equity (OPZ crypto-coins) by creating interesting content.

Social networks are public forums and should be decentralized and user-owned. Decentralization ensures that no single entity can moderate discussions. User ownership removes incentives to collect and monetize user data and to push click-bait advertisements.

On Opozee, user data belongs to the users. For example, if the user community decides to deploy a revenue model on the network, the proceeds will be paid out to OPZ coin holders.

To ensure that Opozee’s network is censorship resistant, we plan to create a fully decentralized organization that is owned and operated by its users, content creators, developers, and moderators.
